# Overview

Healthcare information is often scattered across paper records, patient portals, emails, prescriptions, lab reports, and handwritten notes.

Preparing for appointments, remembering symptom history, understanding medical terminology, and tracking long-term health progress can quickly become overwhelming.

**Rihla+** is an AI-powered personal healthcare companion designed to bring every part of a patient's health journey into one secure, intuitive platform.

Instead of managing fragmented information, users can build a living health timeline, securely store important medical records, prepare for appointments with AI assistance, and gain meaningful insights from their personal health data.

Rihla+ is designed to help individuals become more informed, organized, and confident participants in their own healthcare journey.
