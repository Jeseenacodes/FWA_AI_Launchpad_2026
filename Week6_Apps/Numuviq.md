> Transform business questions into structured investigations, actionable insights, and leadership-ready recommendations.

NUMUVIQ is an analyst platform designed to help Data Analysts, Product Analysts, Business Analysts, and Analytics Leaders approach business problems with a structured, repeatable framework.

Instead of starting with dashboards, SQL queries, or reports, NUMUVIQ helps users define investigation paths, identify key metrics, recommend visualizations, generate stakeholder questions, and create actionable recommendations.

Link:  https://numuviq.lovable.app/ 
---

## Overview

Organizations frequently face questions such as:

* Why did revenue decline?
* Why are customers churning?
* Why is product adoption decreasing?
* What caused conversion rates to drop?
* Which metrics should we investigate?

NUMUVIQ helps teams move from uncertainty to clarity by generating structured investigation plans that support data-driven decision-making.

## Why NUMUVIQ?

Many analytics tools help users analyze data after it has already been collected.

NUMUVIQ focuses on the critical step before analysis begins:

**Problem Framing.**

The platform helps analysts structure their thinking, identify the right questions, and develop a clear investigation strategy before building reports or querying data.
