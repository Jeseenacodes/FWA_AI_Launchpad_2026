## Overview

Product teams collect feedback from multiple sources such as customer reviews, support tickets, surveys, and user interviews. While gathering feedback is relatively easy, determining what to prioritize next is often much more difficult.

Product Discovery Assistant is an AI-powered tool that helps Product Managers and Product Analysts transform customer feedback, product metrics, and business goals into actionable product recommendations.

The application identifies recurring themes, prioritizes opportunities, recommends experiments, and suggests success metrics to support data-informed product decisions.

---

## Problem

When building products, teams frequently ask:

> What should we build next?

Customer feedback is often scattered across different channels and can be difficult to analyze at scale.

Common challenges include:

* Hundreds of customer comments to review
* Competing feature requests
* Limited development resources
* Unclear prioritization criteria
* Difficulty aligning feedback with business goals

As a result, product teams may spend significant time debating priorities instead of making informed decisions.

---

## Solution

Product Discovery Assistant combines:

* Customer Feedback
* Product Metrics
* Business Goals

and uses AI to generate:

### Theme Analysis

Identifies recurring patterns and customer pain points.

### Opportunity Prioritization

Ranks potential improvements based on impact and strategic alignment.

### Executive Summary

Provides a concise explanation of the most important opportunities.

### Experiment Recommendations

Suggests hypotheses and experiments to validate ideas.

### Success Metrics

Recommends primary, secondary, and guardrail metrics to measure outcomes.

## Example Use Cases

### Tamilingo

Business Goal: Increase Engagement

Customer Feedback:

* Need more speaking practice
* Lessons feel repetitive
* Would love pronunciation feedback
* Need real-world conversations

Output:

* Theme: Speaking Practice
* Opportunity: Interactive Conversation Exercises
* Success Metrics: Session Duration, Lesson Completion Rate, Weekly Active Users

---

### Little Noories

Business Goal: Improve Retention

Customer Feedback:

* Need more bedtime stories
* Kids lose interest quickly
* Would love audio narration
* Need interactive learning activities

Output:

* Theme: Content Engagement
* Opportunity: Interactive Audio Storytelling
* Success Metrics: Retention Rate, Story Completion Rate, Return Users

---

### SaaS Product

Business Goal: Reduce Churn

Customer Feedback:

* Dashboard loads too slowly
* Need PDF export
* Mobile experience is difficult
* Reports take too long to generate

Output:

* Theme: Product Performance
* Opportunity: Improve Dashboard Speed
* Success Metrics: Retention Rate, Churn Rate, Session Duration

## Project Inspiration

This project was inspired by challenges encountered while building personal products such as Tamilingo and Little Noories.

While collecting user feedback was straightforward, determining which improvements would create the greatest impact was significantly harder.

Product Discovery Assistant was created to provide a structured, AI-powered approach to product discovery and prioritization.
