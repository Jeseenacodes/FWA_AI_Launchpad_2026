# AI Career, Education & Analytics Assistants

A collection of custom AI assistants designed to solve real-world problems in education, analytics, product strategy, and career development.

These assistants demonstrate how prompt engineering, knowledge grounding, and domain expertise can be combined to create specialized AI systems that go beyond general-purpose chatbots.

---

## Projects
### 1. Next Chapter Analytics Coach 

**Platform:** Claude Project / Custom GPT
https://chatgpt.com/g/g-6a24ebf2e7e08191b83879bda0769643-next-chapter-analytics-coach 

An AI career coach that helps educators transition into data analytics, business intelligence, and reporting roles.

#### Capabilities

* Identify transferable skills
* Resume transformation
* LinkedIn optimization
* Portfolio planning
* Interview preparation
* Learning roadmaps
* Career transition guidance

#### Knowledge Base

* Transferable Skills Guide
* Resume Transformation Examples
* Interview Question Bank
* Analytics Learning Roadmap
* Data Analyst Job Descriptions

#### Example Prompts

```text
Rewrite my teaching experience for a Data Analyst role.
```

```text
Create a 6-month analytics learning roadmap.
```

```text
Conduct a mock Data Analyst interview.
```

Rather than creating generic assistants, each project focuses on solving a specific real-world problem with targeted expertise and actionable guidance.
