# 📊 Data Analyst Career Coach Agent

> An AI-powered career coaching agent built with **Relevance AI** to help aspiring data analysts assess their skills, identify gaps, improve their resumes, and create actionable career development plans.

## 🚀 Overview

Transitioning into Data Analytics can be overwhelming. Aspiring analysts often wonder:

* Am I job-ready?
* What skills should I learn next?
* Is my resume ATS-friendly?
* What projects should I build?
* What should I focus on this week?

The **Data Analyst Career Coach Agent** acts as a personalized AI mentor that evaluates a user's profile and provides practical recommendations to accelerate their career journey.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many aspiring data analysts spend significant time researching learning paths, reviewing resumes, comparing job descriptions, and deciding what to learn next. The process is repetitive, fragmented, and often lacks personalized guidance.

---

## 💡 Solution

The Data Analyst Career Coach Agent analyzes a user's skills, experience, portfolio, and career goals to generate a customized career roadmap.

It also integrates a **Resume Reviewer Tool** that evaluates resumes for Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and BI Analyst roles, providing ATS-focused feedback and improvement suggestions.

---

## 🧪 Example Prompts

### Career Assessment

```text
I am transitioning into Data Analytics.

Skills:
- SQL
- Excel
- Power BI

Goal:
Land my first Data Analyst role.

Assess my readiness and recommend my next steps.
```

---

### Resume Review

```text
Review my resume for entry-level Data Analyst roles.

Provide:
- Resume Score
- ATS Feedback
- Missing Keywords
- Improvement Suggestions
```

---

### Skill Gap Analysis

```text
I want to become a Business Analyst.

Current Skills:
- SQL
- Excel
- Power BI

Identify my skill gaps and recommend what I should learn next.
```
